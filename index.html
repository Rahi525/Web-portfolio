Course Details:

Course code: CSE417/418
Course Title Web Engineering & Lab
Program B.Sc. in CSE
Faculty Faculty of Science and Information Technology (FSIT)
Credit Course Level
Sementer Summer 2021
Credit 3+1 3hours/week
Course Level Level-4 Term-1

Course Objective:

This course aims to provide students a systematic approach to develop a deep understanding of web technologies by providing hands-on experience. Students will be able to learn new technologies, analyze existing web applications/systems, and design new done efficiently after the course.

Course Outcome:

CO1-Have an understanding of different programming techniques, protocols available for development
CO2-Have the abilities to analyze and describe the system requirements
CO3-Taking critical decision regarding client and server-side development
CO4-Be able to evaluate existing systems and build new systems by applying state of the art technology
